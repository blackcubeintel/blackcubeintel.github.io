Introduction


Global Investigations is a Detective Agency & Private Investigators Service based in Los Santos that provides all of our clients, both private and corporate, with a very high level of service and commitment and deliver it to them in the most efficient and professional way possible.

With many years combined investigating experience and over 20 years of official trading, our company has earned an enviable reputation for being a highly professional, diligent and scrupulous detective agency, specialising in Background Checks, Tracing, Covert Surveillance, Bug Sweeping, Vehicle Tracking and Due Diligence investigations.
